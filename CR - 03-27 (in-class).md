## 03-27, in-class assignment

---


~~~{.ProofChecker .JohnsonSL options="fonts tabindent render" guides="fitch" points="17" late-credit="14"}
4.1 P -> Q, R <-> P, P :|-: Q & R
4.2 (T & Q) & R, (T & R) -> S :|-: S
4.3 A <-> (B v C), A & ~C :|-: B
4.4 ~P v ~R, ~R <-> S, P :|-: S & ~R
4.5 (~C v F) -> D, F, ~M :|-: ~M & D 
4.6 (P v S) & T, Q -> ~P, Q <-> T :|-: S
