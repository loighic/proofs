## HW 6: TFL rules

6.1 - 6.5 are worth 2 points each. 6.6 is extra credit and is worth 1 point. 

You have to use `<->E`, `<->ex`, `DN`, `->I`, `&I` and `&E` for these proofs.

---

~~~{.ProofChecker .AllenSL options="fonts tabindent render" guides="fitch" points="2"}
6.1 P <-> Q, P :|-: Q 
6.2 P <-> Q, Q :|-: P
6.3 P :|-: ~~P
6.4 ~~Q :|-: Q
6.5 S & T :|-: R -> T
~~~

~~~{.ProofChecker .AllenSL options="fonts tabindent render" guides="fitch" points="1"}
